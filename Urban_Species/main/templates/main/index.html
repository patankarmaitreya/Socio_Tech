WELCOME TO URBAN SPECIES!

Urban species is a website developed for welfare
and protection of animals living in cities. It aims
to aid people who want to help these animals but
do not know how. The website also helps pet
owners and to be pet owners by showing nearby
veternary cilnics and as such that are required for
various needs of their pets as well as providing
information about how the adoption process for
